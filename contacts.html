About Us

Our Story

Tranquilikey is the brainchild of a to-be-psychologist who is unhappy with the current mental health system.

When my friends or acquaintances needed a referral for anything mental health related, they would (and still do!) come to me. I was sort of everyone's mental health practitioner dealer. 

I know how hard it is to find the right therapist. It takes numerous tries. I wanted to help cut down the number of tries, help narrow the search down, and also make it easy to try again. 

Why "Tranquilikey"?
Tranquili + key. It is a bad attempt at wordplay. I hope to make a service that can help people unlock the gates to tranquility, hence the name. 



The logo is a key made out of a nautilus shell. And why nautilus shell? 
It is a symbol of growth, expansion, and renewal. 
Its spiral symbolizes order amidst the chaos. 

Nautili have inhabited the Earth for more than 500 million years, and I think that's a cool symbol to signify the resilience of everyone who battles their demons everyday.
