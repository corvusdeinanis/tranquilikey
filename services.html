Why Tranquilikey?

Affordable. Accessible. Awesome.

Might not be the catchiest, but these three As make up our core tenet, our guiding philosophy. 

From the web design to everything else, we strive to make everything accessible. 

Open.

As open source enthusiasts and supporters of libre software movement, everything we make is open source! Come take a peek!

From the code that powers our website, to our business model, to everything else. Want to seek clarity regarding something? Feel we haven't been open enough? Let us know and we'll fix it!

Powered by love

We run on love and gratitude. No, really. Our ENTIRE business model is based on gratitude and paying it forward. Might sound silly to some, but the power is with the community. We believe in you <3

We don't sell your data!

As open source enthusiasts and supporters of libre software movement, everything we make is open source! Come take a peek!

Trauma informed, lived experience respecting, and anti pop-psychology

We're not another "quick fix"; we are against the commodification of mental health. "Mental health" apps are doing more damage than good. 

The information age might have granted us access to unlimited information, but it has also led to a rise in armchair psychologists, pop psychology, and usage of clinical terms with no nuance or regard.
